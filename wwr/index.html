Uganda

Uganda is a landlocked country in East Africa known for its natural beauty, diverse wildlife, and rich culture. It has lakes, mountains, rivers, forests, and national parks. The capital city is Kampala. Major languages include English and Luganda. Uganda is called the “Pearl of Africa” for its stunning scenery and biodiversity.
